Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026 | Effective Date: July 2026

1. Introduction

360IT Learning & Consulting ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (mio3.serverafrica.net), enroll in our training bootcamps, request enterprise IT consulting services, or communicate with our team.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal and organization information that you voluntarily provide to us when interacting with our site:

  • Contact Information: Full name, corporate email address, telephone number, job title, and organization name when submitting contact forms, requesting consultations, or subscribing to tech updates.
  • Enrollment & Academic Data: Preferred training course, delivery mode preference (online/onsite), technical background, and course progress data.
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and page view statistics collected automatically via standard server logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Delivering requested IT consulting services, project proposals, and technical audits.
  • Processing training course enrollments, cohort scheduling, and issuing certificates of completion.
  • Sending administrative updates, invoice details, and service alerts.
  • Distributing newsletter content and technical whitepapers (you may opt out at any time).
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations and regulatory standards.

4. Data Protection & Security Controls

We implement enterprise-grade technical and organizational security measures, including SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit, strict database access controls, firewall isolation, and regular vulnerability assessments. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Client Confidentiality & NDAs

For enterprise consulting engagements and government contract delivery, 360IT Learning & Consulting adheres to strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs). All client infrastructure specifications, code repositories, network diagrams, and proprietary algorithms remain strictly confidential.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses essential session cookies to enable core website navigation, secure form submissions, and maintain user preferences. You can disable non-essential cookies via your browser settings, though certain interactive features may be limited.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access and receive a copy of your stored personal data.
  • Right to request correction or updating of inaccurate data.
  • Right to request erasure ("right to be forgotten") of personal records.
  • Right to opt out of promotional communications at any time.
