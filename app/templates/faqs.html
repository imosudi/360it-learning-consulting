Training Bootcamps & Certifications

Are your IT training courses live instructor-led or self-paced?

All 360IT training bootcamps are 100% live instructor-led by active senior enterprise engineers and cloud architects. Classes feature real-time interaction, practical lab demonstrations, and dedicated Q&A sessions. Students also receive lifetime access to class recordings, lab guides, and code repositories.

Do you prepare students for official vendor certification exams?

Yes. Our curriculum is mapped directly to global certification standards including AWS Certified Solutions Architect (SAA-C03), Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator (AZ-104), Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA), and CompTIA Security+. We provide practice voucher guidance, mock exam simulations, and exam strategy workshops.

What prerequisites are needed to enroll in AWS or DevOps bootcamps?

Basic computer literacy and a passion for technology are sufficient for our foundational modules. For intermediate courses like DevOps Engineering and Kubernetes, familiarity with basic Linux command-line syntax and core networking concepts is recommended. Free pre-bootcamp prep modules are provided upon enrollment.

Do you offer flexible payment plans or corporate group discounts?

Yes! We support flexible installment plans for individual learners. For corporate teams of 3 or more employees, we offer tailored enterprise pricing, customized curriculum modules, and private cohort scheduling.

Enterprise IT Consulting & Government Contracts

How does 360IT handle zero-downtime cloud migration engagements?

We utilize a phased 6-stage migration framework: Assessment & Discovery, Cloud Architecture Design, Pilot Proof-of-Concept, Data Synchronization, Cutover Execution, and 24/7 Post-Cutover Monitoring. We guarantee strict Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with zero disruption to core business operations.

Can 360IT deploy consultants for government technology contracts?

Yes. 360IT Learning & Consulting is an authorized technology partner equipped for public sector engagements, government ministry portal modernizations, healthcare infrastructure, and telecommunications upgrades. Our engineers hold security clearances and follow strict NIST/ISO security standards.

What managed IT support packages do you offer?

We offer 24/7/365 proactive infrastructure monitoring, automated server patch management, backup verification, container cluster health checks, and tier-3 incident escalation. Support plans are fully customizable to meet your compliance requirements.
